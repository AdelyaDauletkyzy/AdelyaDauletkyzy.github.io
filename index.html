Geography, Population, Climate

Shymkent is the third major city in Kazakhstan, after Almaty and Astana. It is located in the southern part of Kazakhstan, in Turkestan Region (Oblast). It has the population of almost 1 million people. It consists of variety of ethnicities, including Kazakh, Russian, Uzbek, Turk, and others. It has extremely hot summers and warm winters.
