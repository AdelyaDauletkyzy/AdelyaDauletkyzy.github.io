Basic boring wikipedia information

Geography, Population, Climate

Shymkent is the third major city in Kazakhstan, after Almaty and Astana. It is located in the southern part of Kazakhstan, in Turkestan Region (Oblast). It has the population of almost 1 million people. It consists of variety of ethnicities, including Kazakh, Russian, Uzbek, Turk, and others. It has extremely hot summers and warm winters.

Reasons I like Shymkent

Liking Shymkent is difficult, but here are some reasons I like it:
  • Warm weather & Aquaparks

    It is so warm that I think only in Shymkent in entire Kazakhstan you can enjoy variety of aqua parks

  • Mountains

    Not like in Almaty unfortunately, but at least there are some that can be seen compared to Astana

  • My family is there
    Literally the only reason I still frequently visit the city
  • Everything is cheaper
  • Relaxed and chill people
  • Coffeehouses

    Global Coffee especially; it was first opened in Shymkent and was my life (GPA) saver during quarantine
